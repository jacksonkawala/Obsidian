# Daily Geotechnical Report — 10 April 2026

**Ref:** 081/GTC/2026 | **Date:** 10-04-2026 | **Time:** 06:00–11:00 AM

## Highlights

### WCPC Water Body — RED ALERT (unchanged)
Water level still at **10.10 mRL**, [[Freeboard]] **0.90 m** (Red Alert). Remains non-compliant. **Halt all inflows.**

### Paddock 73 — Continuing Improvement + Seepage Discovery
Water level dropped another 250 mm: **41.75 → 41.50 mRL**. [[Freeboard]] improved to **2.20 m** (up from 1.95 m). However, **seepage daylighting has been observed at the toe of Berm 73** — a new finding that requires close monitoring to verify whether it develops further. This is potentially significant for berm stability.

### Tails 1B — O/F Discharge Now Eroding Berm
The overflow discharge from Tails 1B stacker is now eroding Berm 75 (previously not eroding on 09 Apr). Washing and roping observed at the underflow. Still assessed as safe to operate.

### TSF — Another Liner Stolen, Pool Continuing to Drop
Yet another section of the TSF starter wall liner has been stolen, continuing the pattern of theft-reinstatement-theft. The pool level has dropped to **24.60 mRL** (down 0.15 m from 24.75), confirming the pump restoration is working. The report text also references a reading of **24.30 mRL** — if confirmed, this would represent a further 0.30 m drop. Seepage along the toe of the second lift continues. The slimes pipeline hanging at the starter wall bend remains a progressive collapse risk.
