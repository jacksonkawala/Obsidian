## Data Quality Notes

Several internal inconsistencies and suspected copy-paste errors were identified in this report:

1. **TSF "Previous Day" error:** The report lists TSF WL previous day as 23.35 mRL (change +0.95 m). But the 14 Apr geotech report recorded TSF WL at 24.35 mRL. The 23.35 value appears to be from the 12–13 Apr timeframe. The true day-on-day change is approximately -0.05 m (24.35→24.30), not +0.95 m.
2. **PDP-1 "Previous Day" mismatch:** The report shows 34.30 mRL as the previous day value, but the 14 Apr geotech recorded PDP-1 at 34.70 mRL. If the 14 Apr reading was correct, the WL actually dropped 0.30 m (34.70→34.40), not rose 0.10 m as reported.
3. **Float-off Pond data appears to be copy-paste from 14 Apr:** Identical values — Today 9.10, Previous 9.80, Change -0.70. If the level was 9.10 on 14 Apr, the "Previous Day" column on 15 Apr should read 9.10, not 9.80. The true change may be approximately zero.
