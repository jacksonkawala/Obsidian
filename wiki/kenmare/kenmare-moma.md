# Kenmare Moma Mining (Mauritius) Limited

Heavy mineral sands mining operation at Moma, Mozambique. Also operates as Kenmare Moma Processing (Mauritius) Limited.

## Facilities — Water Containment & Processing

- **[[Nataka TSF]]** — Tailings Storage Facility for slimes deposition (Phase 1, Lift 1 in progress)
- **WCP-A Dredge Pond** — Active dredge mining pond with 4 mining faces (Calen, Sandra, Port Side, Starboard)
- **WCPA** — Water Containment and Processing Area (includes TSF, PDP, WCP-A)
- **WCPC** — Water body currently at RED ALERT (freeboard 0.90 m as of 08 Apr 2026)
- **PDP-1** — Primary Densification Pond 1 (water level 34.40 mRL)
- **PDP-2** — Primary Densification Pond 2 (water level 31.90 mRL)
- **Finishing Pond-C** — feeds into WCP-A
- **RAW Dam** — make-up and main line water supply
- **Exit Pond** — discharge point (currently at max allowable, turbidity non-compliant)
- **Float-off Pond** — erosion issues observed
- **SMO** — Surface Mining Operations area (berm breach risk — tailings encroaching, slumps developing, sea wall erosion from stacker washouts)
- **HTC** — buildup risk of backflow toward Mualadi Lake

## Facilities — Paddocks

- **Paddock 73** — ORANGE ALERT (continuing improvement — freeboard 2.20 m, water level 41.50 mRL, Godwin pump at max capacity). NEW: seepage daylighting at toe of Berm 73 observed 10 Apr.
- **Paddock 72** — Active (freeboard 2.40 m, water level 50.90 mRL)
- **Paddock 61** — Emergency pipes burnt, overflow risk
- **Paddocks 69, 58/68, 66, 60** — Water level building up, siphoning required
- **ISOA Paddock** — On the limit (freeboard 2.30 m, liner reinstatement needed)
- **External Paddock 7** — Water level building up, siphoning required
- **Old DM Pit** — ORANGE ALERT (exceeds max allowable by 0.10 m)

## Facilities — Tails Stackers

- **Tails 1A** — Starboard sidewall WCPA (safe to operate)
- **Tails 1B** — Port sidewall WCPA (safe to operate; O/F discharge now eroding berm as of 10 Apr)
- **Tails 2A** — Berm 75 (safe to operate as of 09 Apr — was NOT safe on 08 Apr)
- **Tails 2A O/E** — Paddock 72 (safe to operate)
- **Tails 3A** — NW Valley 3 (erosion from U/F, exacerbated by rainfall)

## Valleys

- **Valley 3 Embankment** — disturbed during infrastructure removal, further disturbed by rainfall. Reinstatement in progress.
- **Namalope West Valley 1 (upstream)** — water level 25.70 mRL, approaching/exceeding initial allowable

## Active Site-Wide Issues (10 Apr 2026)

- **Liner theft** — recurring issue across PDP-1, PDP-2, and TSF starter wall. Another TSF starter wall section stolen on 10 Apr. Liners are being ripped off shortly after reinstatement.
- **Rainfall-induced erosion** — widespread across access roads, berms, embankments, and sidewalls following 200 mm rainfall on 02 Apr. PDP-2 starboard erosion getting worse.
- **WCPC RED ALERT** — freeboard 0.90 m, halt all inflows.
- **SMO berm breach risk** — tailings encroaching on separation berm with slumps developing. Stacker washouts eroding sea wall.
- **TSF water return restored** — 16,249 m³/d now returning to pond (was zero on 08 Apr). Pool level dropping.
- **3A stacker washouts damaging Lift 1** — stacker relocated, retention berms under construction.
- **Paddock 73 berm seepage** — seepage daylighting at toe of Berm 73 observed 10 Apr. Potential stability concern.
- **Tails 1B O/F eroding berm** — new as of 10 Apr, was not eroding previously.
