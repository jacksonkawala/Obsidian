### Critical Issues

- **3A stacker washouts** — the prolonged washouts have led to wet tailings encroaching on Lift 1 construction, eroding the sidewall and compromising structural integrity. Risks include flooded access roads restricting HME movement, erosion of the compacted wall, and liquefied sand tailings immobilising equipment. Following a field meeting, the stacker has been stopped and relocated. Retention berms are being built at the new position. Affected areas to be remediated (remove wet tailings, recompact, retest). Road access restricted near operating stackers.
- **Erosion on exposed starter wall** — rainfall has initiated surface erosion and material loss on the section where liner was previously stolen. Urgent rehabilitation and liner reinstatement recommended.
- **Erosion of Lift 1 wall** from recent rainfall — reinstatement required.
- **Runoff accumulating** between Valley 3 and TSF toe berms — siphoning underway, monitoring developments per EoR agreement.
- **QC equipment** for wall lift construction identified, procurement in progress.
- **TPH lab services** on site assisting with test tests and data capturing.
