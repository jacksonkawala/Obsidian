## Lift 1 Construction

Construction remains suspended — zero fill on both day and night shifts. HMEs continue reestablishing access roads following intensive rainfall.

Key issues:
- TPH lab services on site assisting with test tests and data capturing; all testing point collars must be surveyed and recorded.
- Runoff accumulating between Valley 3 and TSF toe berms — siphoning underway per EoR agreement.
- QC equipment for wall lift construction identified; procurement ongoing.
- Sand around drainage outlet needs cleaning.
- Liner reinstatement required on cut sections — rainfall-induced erosion observed.
- Erosion of Lift 1 wall by recent rainfall — reinstatement required.

**3A stacker washout update:** Prolonged washouts from the 3A stacker caused wet tailings to encroach on Lift 1, eroding the sidewall and compromising structural integrity. Risks include flooding of access roads, erosion of compacted wall, and immobilisation of HMEs in liquefied sand tailings. Following a field meeting, the stacker was relocated (completed), retention berms are under construction, and affected areas will be remediated (remove wet tailings, recompact, retest).

**Starter wall erosion:** Rainfall-induced erosion observed on exposed sections where the liner was removed/stolen. Urgent rehabilitation and liner reinstatement recommended.
