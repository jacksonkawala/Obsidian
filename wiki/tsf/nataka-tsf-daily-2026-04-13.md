## Critical Observations

### Freeboard Recovery Reversed
After climbing from 5.25 m (08 Apr) to 6.65 m (12 Apr), freeboard dropped sharply by 1.00 m to 5.65 m on 13 Apr. The pool rose from 23.35 to 24.35 mRL in a single day. This reversal occurred despite all four pumps operating at 100% availability and returning 3,638 m³/d to the pond.

> **Cross-report discrepancy:** The 13 Apr geotech report (Ref 083/GTC/2026, inspected 06:00–11:00 on 13 Apr) recorded TSF WL at 23.35 mRL and freeboard at 6.65 m. This TSF daily summary, which covers the full day of 13 Apr, reports 24.35 mRL and 5.65 m. A 1.0 m discrepancy for the same date. The morning reading may have been correct at inspection time, with the pool rising significantly during the remainder of the day. See [[Daily Geotechnical Report — 13 April 2026]].

### Pump Return Collapsed
Water returned from TSF to pond dropped to 3,638 m³/d (down from 16,249 m³/d on 09 Apr). All four pumps remain at 100% availability, so this reduction may reflect operational throttling, lower pump head, or pipeline constraints rather than equipment failure.

### Zero Slimes Received
The plant remained shut on 13 Apr. No slimes were received at the TSF (average density 0 t/m³, total slimes received 0 m³).
