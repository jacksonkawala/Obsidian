## Capacity Model Revision

The 14 Apr report significantly revised the TSF capacity model:

| Parameter | 13 Apr | 14 Apr | Change |
|-----------|--------|--------|--------|
| Storage space available | 741,000 t | **1,200,000 t** | +62% |
| Solids mass to date | 678,532 t | 680,614 t | +2,082 t |
| Remaining capacity | 62,458 t | 519,386 t | +456,928 t |
| Remaining days | 53 | 97 | +44 days |
| Phase 1 capacity date | 05 Jun 2026 | 20 Jul 2026 | +45 days |
| TSF % filled | 92% | 57% | -35 pp |

The 14 Apr report notes the revised capacity is "based on assumed dry density and calibrated models" and flags that a **survey is required for verification**. The previous 741,000 t figure was the original Phase 1 design volume; the 1,200,000 t likely incorporates Lift 1 raise volume at an assumed in-situ dry density.

This is a materially important change — the apparent urgency dropped from 53 to 97 days remaining. However, the revised figure is unverified and contingent on Lift 1 completion. Until a bathymetric survey confirms the actual available volume, the original capacity timeline should be treated as the conservative baseline.

## Critical Observations

### Plant Restarted
After several days of zero slimes input, 13,049 m³ of slurry was received on 14 Apr (avg density 1.10 t/m³). Total solids received to date: 680,614 t.

### Pool Level Stabilising
The pool dropped marginally from 24.35 to 24.30 mRL (-0.05 m). Freeboard recovered slightly to 5.70 m (from 5.65 m). The sharp 1.00 m rise between 12 and 13 Apr has stabilised.

### Water Return Restored to High Volume
Water returned TSF→Pond surged to 15,592.53 m³/d (up from 3,638 on 13 Apr — a fourfold increase). All four pumps remain at 100% availability.

### Finishing Pond C Now Pumping to WCPA
A new inflow of 16,800 m³/d from Finishing Pond C to WCP-A has been established (was 0 on 13 Apr). This adds to the already positive WCPA balance.
